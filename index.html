Fullstack Web Development

Learn to build web applications and mobile applications and to add domains and to add your apps to Google Play Store. Full-stack development is a comprehensive approach to building web applications, encompassing both the front-end (user interface) and back-end (server-side logic and database). A full-stack developer is someone proficient in both aspects, able to work on the entire development lifecycle. They design and create websites and applications for various platforms, from user experience to server-side database management, according to Coursera.

Data Science

Learn how to analyze data using power BI and python and to organize large data sets. AI Overview Data scientists use various techniques like data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization to uncover patterns and insights within data. Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines principles and practices from various disciplines like mathematics, statistics, computer science, and domain expertise to extract knowledge and insights from data. It focuses on understanding and interpreting data to solve real-world problems and make informed decisions.

Artificial Intelligence

AI deals with making machines that think like human beings and perform tasks.They are fed data and trained. Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, such as reasoning, learning, and problem-solving. AI aims to simulate human intelligence by using algorithms to analyze data, identify patterns, and make decisions based on those patterns. It's a rapidly evolving field with applications across various industries, from healthcare and finance to transportation and education
